ABOUT OUR PRODUCT

Who We Are

Cocofert is a producer and supplier of high-quality cocopeat products, catering to the growing demands of the horticulture and agriculture industries. We leverage sustainable practices and innovative technologies to harness the potential of coconut husks, providing eco-friendly solutions for plant growth and development.

Our Mission

To produce and deliver exceptional cocopeat products that enhance plant growth, reduce environmental impact, and promote sustainable agriculture practices.

Our Vision

To become the global benchmark for sustainable cocopeat products, empowering farmers, growers, and eco-conscious consumers to cultivate a greener future. We are committed to revolutionizing plant growth with eco-friendly practices while fostering a community dedicated to sustainability and environmental stewardship.
